Meet my most cherished companions!

Zuko

Fire Lord Zuko, my beloved nephew, is dear to me like a son. Although his journey has been filled with pain and hardship, he has blossomed into a remarkable young leader — for he has discovered true strength comes from kindness and wisdom.

Aang

Master of all four elements, Avatar Aang helped bring peace among the four nations. I deeply admire the young avatar's ability to find joy in the simplest of moments, even with the weight of the universe on his shoulders.

Toph

Toph, the fierce earthbender, has succeeded not in spite of her blindness but precisely because of it. This potential obstacle enabled her to see the world in a unique way, teaching us that limitations are nothing but mere illusions.

Katara

Katara of the Southern Water Tribe has developed into the finest waterbender I have ever known. Her transformation from a frightened child to a formidable protector illustrates her unwavering courage and unyielding compassion.

It is important to draw wisdom from many different places. If we take it from only one place, it becomes rigid and stale.
— Uncle Iroh
